say

The first bot of the group say something around it.

Parameters :
[tell|say|shout|universe|escort]: <phrase_id>

'escort' is used to send the message to the player member of the team escorting the bots groups.

'tell' is used to send the message to the only one player that just targeted or followed the bot. You can use the 'tell' mode only under a 'player_target_npc' or 'player_follow_npc' event.
